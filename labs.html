High Temperature Gasdynamics Laboratory

The High Temperature Gasdynamics Laboratory (HTGL) is an interdisciplinary teaching and research group, combining aspects of atomic and molecular physics, lasers and electro-optics, physical chemistry and electromagnetic phenomena, together with the traditional mechanical engineering disciplines of fluid mechanics, heat transfer and thermodynamics. The HTGL has several associated laboratories:

  • Advanced Laser Diagnostics and Shock Tube Lab (R.K. Hanson)
  • Nanomaterials Synthesis Lab (X. Zheng)
  • Plasma Physics Lab (M.A. Cappelli)
  • Advanced Propulsion Lab (C.T. Bowman)
  • Advanced Energy Systems Lab (C.F. Edwards)
  • Heterogeneous Combustion Lab (R.E. Mitchell)


Microscale Science Laboratories

  • Microfluidics Lab (J.G. Santiago)
  • NanoHeat Lab (K.E. Goodson)
  • Tang Lab (S. Tang)
