Maps and Driving Directions

Map of the San Francisco Bay Area

Vicinity map of the Stanford and Palo Alto areas

Detailed map of campus and the Thermosciences Group buildings (500, 520 and 570)


From Highway 101 North and South
Exit on University Avenue west towards Stanford University. Follow University Avenue through Palo Alto and onto campus where University Avenue becomes Palm Drive. Continue on Palm Drive until reaching the Oval, at the front of the Main Quad. Visitor Parking is available at the meters. Walk across the Quad to Buildings 520, 530, 570 and 660 which are across the road behind the church.


From Highway 280 North and South
Exit on Page Mill Road east towards Stanford University. Follow Page Mill Road to Junipero Serra and turn left. At Stanford Avenue, turn right and follow until Bowdoin Avenue. Turn left on Bowdoin, entering campus. At Campus Drive, turn right and follow Campus Drive to Palm Drive. Turn left on Palm Drive and continue until you reach the Oval.
